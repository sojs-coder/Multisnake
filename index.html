Welcome to MultiSnake...

How to play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move
  • Plays like standard Snake game
  • Multiplayer
  • Goal is to get to 50 points
  • Points are gained by eating apples
  • SPACE to use boost
  • Boost burns up apple
